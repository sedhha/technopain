OPTIMIZING EXPOSURE TO INTERGALACTIC RADIATIONS IN FLIGHTS TRAVELING FROM POLES


In Nasa space apps 2017, I worked on a project where the aim was to reduce the overall intergalactic radiation dosage using smart materials and algorithms. As a team of 3 people, we used the NASA database to create a functional relationship between a given altitude to which the airplane is flying with penetration power of cosmic rays to that particular altitude, based on this the flight algorithms could be altered to the safest path.





WHEGGED ROBOT


A robot which can have the features of wheels, as well as the legs, is whegged robot. We as a team of three created single actuated moving robot which could easily be used to walk over any terrains. The robot had its self-balancing tail which prevented it from toppling. In addition to this, the design was made small and compact so that it could easily be moved and tested.





COMPUTER VISION AND INSTRUMENTATION TECHNIQUES IN SOIL INSPECTION


Using modern-day microcontrollers and microprocessors embedded with environmental sensors and a small digital camera, I embedded them together to give their combined data and infer it into meaningful information. Using all this a simple dnn algorithm is used to predict the best-suited crop along with the required resources.

SYNTHESIS OF NANOFLUIDS FOR LUBRICATION SYSTEM IN HEAVY MACHINERY

In this project, the focus was majorly on two properties of nanofluid:


  1. Temperature Variation
    2. and
  2. Lubrication.

The nanofluid was synthesized using Aluminium oxide powder and two different solvents were used to have a wide range of comparison.

Once the nanofluid was prepared, a set of spur gear driven by a DC motor of 10 rpm was used and lubrication was applied to observe the wearing and friction effects.

GENERATIVE DESIGN AND SIMULATION TO OBTAIN MOST EFFICIENT AUTOMOBILE STRUCTURE

Generative Design is basically designing performed by a computer, based on some selective parameters which enable the user, to constraint them and perform iterations by intuitive AI to obtain creative, new and designs beyond human imagination.

In this project, I fixed certain parameters like the frontal area, overall weight, ground clearance, wheel diameter, etc. Since the generative design concept is new to SolidWorks, I used SolidWorks equation modeler to change various parameters without breaking the constraints.

After varying equation parameters and generating 15 designs, they all were imported in Ansys to perform structural simulation and computational fluid dynamics to obtain the fittest design of all.
